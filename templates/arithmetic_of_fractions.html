Arithmetic of Fractions

When learning how to do things in maths it is always a good idea to insert easy numbers into your equations to check everything is working as you expect.

We will start with multiplication of fractions. Imagine a circular pizza. If we cut it in half and eat one half we are left with a half. Numerically we can express this as $1 \times 1/2 = 1/2$

Now, if we cut the half into half and eat one half we are left with a quarter. Numerically we can express this as $1/2 \times 1/2 = 1/4$
